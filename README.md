# 🌸 Sakhi AI

## Project Vision

Sakhi AI is an AI-powered Women's Health & Hygiene Education application designed to educate girls and women, especially in rural and semi-urban areas.

The application provides trustworthy, multilingual, and easy-to-understand information about:

- First Period
- Menstrual Health
- Menstrual Hygiene
- Personal Hygiene
- Intimate Hygiene
- Puberty
- Nutrition
- Mental Health
- Women's Health
- Emergency Awareness

The goal is to make every girl feel safe, confident, and informed through AI-powered conversations, educational videos, voice guidance, and simple language.
